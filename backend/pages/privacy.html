Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

POD Design Generator ("we", "our", "us") is a Shopify application that helps merchants create AI-generated product designs and publish them to their Shopify stores. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

Store Information

When you install our app, we collect:

  • Your Shopify store domain (e.g., yourstore.myshopify.com)
  • A Shopify access token (used to create products on your behalf)
  • Your granted OAuth scopes

Design Data

We store the design prompts you enter, AI-generated images, and product listing data you create within the app. This data is associated with your store domain.

API Keys

If you provide third-party API keys (e.g., OpenAI, KIE.ai, Printful), these are stored securely and used solely to process your design requests.

Usage Data

We track basic usage metrics such as the number of designs created and products published, for plan enforcement and analytics.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Customer personal data (names, emails, addresses, payment info)
  • Order details or customer transaction data
  • Browsing behavior of your store's visitors

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI artwork and product mockups based on your prompts
  • To publish products to your Shopify store via the Shopify API
  • To manage your subscription and enforce plan usage limits
  • To provide customer support

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share data with:

  • AI service providers (OpenAI, KIE.ai, Stability AI) — only design prompts and images, as needed to generate your designs
  • Printful — only product type information, for mockup generation
  • Shopify — product data you choose to publish to your store

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway. Access tokens are stored server-side and never exposed to the frontend. All communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as the app is installed. When you uninstall the app:

  • Your access token is immediately invalidated
  • All store data (designs, settings, assets) is permanently deleted within 48 hours, in compliance with Shopify's data protection requirements

You may request data deletion at any time by contacting us.

8. GDPR Compliance

We comply with GDPR and Shopify's mandatory data protection webhooks:

  • Customer data request — We do not store customer personal data
  • Customer data erasure — We do not store customer personal data
  • Shop data erasure — All shop data is purged upon request

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we store about your shop
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Uninstall the app at any time from your Shopify admin

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via the app or email.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Shopify App Store listing or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
